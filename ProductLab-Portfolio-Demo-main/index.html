WHAT I DO...

PRODUCT MANAGER

As a product manager, I spearhead the product lifecycle from inception to completion. My role involves understanding market needs, liaising between technical and non-technical teams to translate those needs into product features, and overseeing the overall execution. I use data-driven decisions and employ strategic planning to ensure the product aligns with our business objectives and resonates with the end-users.

PRODUCT DESIGN

As a Product Designer, my role spans envisioning user-centric design concepts, crafting seamless user interfaces, and iterating designs based on user feedback and data. I continually strive to simplify and enhance the user experience by deeply understanding the users' needs and translating them into tangible product features.
